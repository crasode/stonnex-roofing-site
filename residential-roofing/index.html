Common Questions

Residential Roofing
FAQs

How long does a residential roof replacement take?
Most single-family homes are completed in one to two days. Larger homes or complex roof configurations may take three days. We'll confirm the schedule before we start.
Do you do a full tear-off or can you roof over existing shingles?
We always recommend a full tear-off. Roofing over existing layers traps moisture, adds weight to your structure, and shortens the lifespan of your new roof. We inspect the decking after tear-off and replace any damaged boards before installation.
What warranty do you provide?
Every project includes a written workmanship warranty from Stonnex plus the manufacturer's material warranty, which ranges from 25 to 50 years depending on the product selected.
Do you handle permits?
Permit requirements vary by municipality. We advise on whether a permit is required for your project and can assist with the application process where needed.
How much does a residential roof replacement cost?
Cost depends on roof size, pitch, material selection, and current deck condition. We provide free, detailed written quotes with no obligation. Book an inspection to get an accurate number for your home.
