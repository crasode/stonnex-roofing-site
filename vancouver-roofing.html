Common Questions

Roofing in Vancouver
Answered.

How much does a roof replacement cost in Vancouver?
Roof replacement in Vancouver typically ranges from $12,000 to $28,000 depending on home size, pitch, existing deck condition, and material selection. Asphalt architectural shingles represent the most common choice. Stonnex provides written fixed-price quotes after a complimentary on-site inspection — no estimates over the phone.
Do you replace flat roofs in Vancouver?
Yes. Stonnex installs and replaces torch-on membrane, modified bitumen, and TPO flat roofing systems across Vancouver including condos, strata townhomes, and detached homes with low-slope rooflines.
How long does roof replacement take in Vancouver?
Most standard Vancouver residential roof replacements are completed in one to two days. Larger homes or complex pitches may require three days. Flat roof systems are typically completed in one to two days depending on system type.
Is Stonnex licensed to work in Vancouver?
Yes. Stonnex holds all required BC licensing, is WorkSafeBC registered, and carries $5M general liability insurance. Our lead installers hold SkilledTradesBC certification in roofing.
