Why Choose Stonnex

What Sets Us Apart —
And Protects You

Most homeowners only discover what their roofer didn't have after something goes wrong. Here is exactly what Stonnex carries on every project — and what each one means for your home, your warranty, and your peace of mind.

WorkSafeBC Registered Employer
Every Stonnex project carries active WorkSafeBC clearance — meaning our workers are fully covered and you face zero liability if an injury occurs on your property. You can verify our standing at worksafebc.com before we start a single nail. Contractors without this leave homeowners legally exposed.
$5,000,000 Commercial General Liability
Our CGL policy covers property damage, bodily injury, and consequential damage for the full duration of every project. We provide the certificate of insurance before work begins — always. Many roofers carry minimal or no coverage; if something goes wrong, you pay. With Stonnex, you never have to find out the hard way.
Licensed BC Contractor & Bonded
Stonnex holds an active BC business licence and contractor registration. Our bonding means that if we ever fail to complete a contracted scope, you have legal financial recourse. This is the baseline every homeowner should require — and most never ask for. We offer it upfront.
BC Building Code Compliance — All Projects
Every installation meets or exceeds the BC Building Code and applicable municipal requirements. Where permits are required, we pull them — we never ask you to. Code-compliant work protects your home's resale value, your insurance coverage, and your family's safety. Non-compliant work voids both.
Written Warranty — Labour & Materials
Every Stonnex project is backed by a written workmanship warranty covering our labour, plus manufacturer warranties on all materials. You receive both documents at project completion, in plain language, with clear terms. Verbal promises from a contractor mean nothing. Written warranties do.
Itemised Written Quotes — Fixed Price
Our quotes list every material, every labour item, and every cost. The price you sign is the price you pay — no change orders for things we missed during our own inspection, no hidden fees on completion. We have never once billed a client more than the signed contract. That record matters to us.
Manufacturer-Approved Materials Only
We install only from approved manufacturer product lines — Owens Corning, IKO, Malarkey, and others appropriate to each application. No off-brand substitutions, no grey-market imports. The materials we specify in your quote are the materials we install. You can check the delivery receipts yourself.
