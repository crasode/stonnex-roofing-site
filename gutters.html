Why It Matters

What Failing Gutters
Actually Cost You

Gutters are the first line of defence against BC's rainfall. When they fail — or were never properly installed — the damage works its way into every part of your home.

01
Foundation Damage Starts Underground
Misdirected water saturates the soil around your foundation. Over time, hydrostatic pressure causes cracking, shifting, and basement infiltration. Foundation repair in BC averages $10,000–$40,000.
02
Fascia and Soffit Rot Within Seasons
Overflowing gutters direct water against your fascia boards and soffit. Wood rot spreads faster than most homeowners expect — and once it reaches structural framing, costs escalate sharply.
03
Landscape and Driveway Erosion
Without properly directed drainage, water carves channels through landscaping and undermines concrete and paving. What looks cosmetic often signals compromised grading and drainage infrastructure.
04
Interior Water Infiltration
Failed or missing gutters allow water to pond against siding and window frames. Seals degrade, water infiltrates wall cavities, and mould establishes. A gutter system costing hundreds prevents remediation costing thousands.
05
Pest Entry Points Open Up
Debris-clogged or rotted gutters attract nesting insects, rodents, and birds. Gaps in deteriorated fascia become access points into your attic — a problem that compounds year over year without intervention.
06
Insurance Claims Get Complicated
Many BC home insurance policies include maintenance exclusions. Water damage resulting from neglected or failed gutters can be classified as a preventable maintenance issue — leaving you responsible for repair costs.

Gutter Systems We Install

The Right System
for Every Home.

We supply and install seamless aluminum, copper, and steel gutter systems — fabricated on-site for a precise, leak-resistant fit on your property.

Seamless Aluminum (K-Style)
The most popular choice for BC homes. Fabricated on-site with no seams along the run — eliminating the most common leak point. Lightweight, rust-resistant, and available in 20+ colours.
Warranty: up to 20 years
Half-Round Aluminum
A traditional profile suited to heritage homes, craftsman architecture, and premium residential builds. Offers the same seamless fabrication with a distinct rounded aesthetic.
Warranty: up to 20 years
Copper Gutters
Premium-grade option for luxury and heritage properties. Develops a natural patina over time, requires no painting, and carries an exceptional lifespan when properly installed.
Lifespan: 50+ years
Steel Gutters
Higher structural rigidity than aluminum — recommended for homes with heavy debris loads, overhanging trees, or high-snowfall exposure. Available in galvanized or galvalume finish.
Warranty: up to 25 years
Leaf Guard & Protection
Micro-mesh or solid cover systems that prevent debris buildup without blocking water flow. Particularly effective in wooded areas of the Lower Mainland where seasonal debris volume is high.
Warranty: system-dependent
