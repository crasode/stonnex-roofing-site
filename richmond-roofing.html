Common Questions

Roofing in Richmond
Answered.

What does roof replacement cost in Richmond?
Richmond residential roof replacement ranges from $10,500 to $22,000 for pitched roofs and $8,000 to $20,000 for flat-roof replacements depending on system type and area. Stonnex provides written fixed-price quotes after a complimentary on-site inspection.
Do you specialize in flat roofs in Richmond?
Yes. Flat and low-slope roofing systems are a core competency for Stonnex. We install torch-on modified bitumen and TPO membrane systems appropriate for Richmond's rainfall intensity and flat-terrain drainage requirements.
How often should a flat roof be inspected in Richmond?
Annual inspection is recommended for flat roofs in Richmond given the city's rainfall volume. We also recommend inspection after any major wind event or after periods of standing water. Stonnex provides complimentary initial inspections.
Does Stonnex work with property managers in Richmond?
Yes. We regularly work with strata councils and property management companies across Richmond, providing written documentation, progress communication, and completion reports that meet strata filing requirements.
