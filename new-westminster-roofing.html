Common Questions

What does roof replacement cost in New Westminster?
New Westminster residential roof replacement typically ranges from $11,000 to $25,000 depending on home size, age, existing deck condition, and material selection. Older homes may carry higher deck repair costs. Stonnex quotes comprehensively after a full on-site inspection.
Does Stonnex work on heritage homes in New Westminster?
Yes. Heritage and character home roofing is a regular part of our New Westminster work. We assess the structural condition of the deck, recommend appropriate modern materials, and execute the replacement with care for the home's character.
How do I know when my New Westminster roof needs replacing?
Signs include granule loss in gutters, curling or lifting shingles, moss growth, visible sagging, or interior water staining. Homes over 20 years in New Westminster should receive a professional inspection.
Does Stonnex offer storm damage inspections in New Westminster?
Yes. Storm and wind damage inspections are available with priority scheduling for active or urgent situations. We provide written reports and photographic documentation for insurance purposes.
